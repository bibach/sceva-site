Event Information

Monthly Meetings

SCEVA members and guests typically meet on the third Saturday of each month for a "meet and greet" gathering. These usually take place at a restaurant in mid-to-late morning. While some club-related announcements might be made, the rest of the time is generally left for whatever topics attendees care to discuss as a group, or in multiple smaller groups. Impromptu show-and-tell sessions are common!

Cars and Coffee

This is a monthly car show organized by another group, which invites the SCEVA to be their featured club, a couple of times each year.

The event usually takes place at the Dunkin' on Hwy A1A in Indialantic, Florida, on a Saturday morning. The location features four 120kW FPL EVolution fast chargers with both CCS and CHAdeMO plugs, should you need to "fill up" while at the event.

All makes and models are welcome. There is no judging or prizes, so just bring what you've got to show off and have fun with fellow car enthusiasts of all varieties!

National Drive Electric Earth Month (NDEEM)

For the last several years, the SCEVA has partnered with the American Muscle Car Museum in Melbourne, Florida to put on this incredible event.

Dealers and representatives of leading EV manufacturers often bring their latest models for attendees to explore and maybe test drive. The Museum also opens up their private test track, so attendees can ride shotgun with a professional driver in a variety of high-performance electric vehicles.

Other booths at the event might include:

  • tinting and other automotive customization and detailing services,
  • sales and rental companies for electric mobility products (e.g., e-bikes and scooters),
  • solar power installation and service companies (a fine complement to the Museum's impressive solar installation!),
  • non-profit and municipal organizations involved in promoting the adoption of electrified transportation and other sustainability intiatives, and
  • FOOD TRUCKS, of course!

The event has previously featured raffles to raise funds for charitable causes, in which attendees may choose to participate. We also highly encourage taking the opportunity to tour the Museum's impressive car collection and other displays.
