Wicked Garden Gnomes

Look out for Gnomes!

Playing familiar-but-uncommon classic rock from the 60's to the 90's, this four-piece band delivers outstanding vocals, rich harmonies, and skilled musicianship. With concert-quality sound and lighting, our professional shows keep our many loyal fans entertained all night long!

We are the sound track to your good time, with songs from artists like The Alan Parsons Project, The Beatles, Blue Öyster Cult, The Cars, Cheap Trick, Chris Isaak, Eagles, Electric Light Orchestra, Foo Fighters, Genesis, Journey, Led Zeppelin, The Moody Blues, Pink Floyd, The Police, REO Speedwagon, Rush, Steve Miller, Styx, Tom Petty and The Who.
