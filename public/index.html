About Space Coast Electric Vehicle Association(SCEVA)

The SCEVA Chapter is a group of electric vehicle enthusiasts in the Brevard County, FL area who enjoy sharing information and learning more about electric vehicles. As a member of the Space Coast EVA, you will have opportunities to share your stories/knowledge and learn from other EV drivers/enthusiasts. You can also experience new and different EVs through various events and meetings.

On a National EVA level, you can join one of the EVA committees to use your skills to make a larger impact. National EVA Member benefits also include educational opportunities, including virtual meetings and our EV Educate™ Certificate Program and EV Blog. See the National Member Benefits for more discounts and membership perks.

Become a member of our local chapter by signing up on the National EVA website.
